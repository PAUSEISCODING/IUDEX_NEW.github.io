CONSEQUENCE Anti‑Cheat

A behavioral, event‑driven anti‑cheat engine designed for modern competitive environments. CONSEQUENCE analyzes player actions, timing patterns, and environmental signals to detect anomalies with near‑zero performance impact. Built for modularity and rapid iteration.

Anti‑Lag Engine

A network‑level optimization system engineered for Minecraft servers. The Anti‑Lag Engine stabilizes tick rates, balances load across distributed environments, and eliminates latency spikes through predictive scheduling and adaptive throttling.

DDoS Defender

DDoS Defender is a high‑resilience mitigation layer engineered to protect online infrastructure from volumetric, protocol‑level, and application‑layer attacks. It continuously analyzes inbound traffic, identifies abnormal patterns, and isolates malicious requests before they reach critical systems. Built with adaptive filtering, rate‑limiting, and intelligent routing, DDoS Defender ensures uninterrupted service availability even under extreme load.

Era Games Studio

Era Games Studio is the creative division of IUDEX INDUSTRIES, focused on building immersive, technically ambitious game experiences. The studio explores new rendering techniques, physics systems, and interactive storytelling methods while maintaining the same engineering discipline found in our security products. Era Games Studio expands IUDEX into the entertainment sector, allowing us to prototype new technologies and push the boundaries of real‑time simulation
